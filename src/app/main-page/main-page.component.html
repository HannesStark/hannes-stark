Guided Research Computer Vision

Niessnerlab at TUM

March 2020 - Sept 2020 Munich, Germany

We used Neural Radiance Fields to render novel views of a scene containing a human. These radiance fields can capture a scene with a low amount of images and then render images from a new unseen viewpoint that did not exist in the original images. Our method added the ability to also change the pose of the human such that you can, for instance, make him walk. Check out the project or the paper .
